# ClipVoBooster - Quick Start Guide

## 🎯 Getting Started in 5 Minutes

### Step 1: Sign Up
1. Go to `https://clipvo.site`
2. Click "Get Started Free" or "Sign In"
3. Connect with your Google account
4. Complete your profile

### Step 2: Complete Your Profile
**Important**: Fill out these fields for best results:
- **Project Name**: Your company/product name
- **Project Description**: What your product does (be specific!)
- **Project URL**: Your website URL (this will be included in emails)

### Step 3: Add Contacts
1. Go to **Contacts** in dashboard
2. Click "Add Contact"
3. Enter:
   - Name
   - Email address
   - Company (optional)
   - Any notes about them

### Step 4: Compose Your First Email
1. Go to **Compose Email**
2. Select a contact or enter email manually
3. AI will auto-generate a professional email using your profile
4. Review and customize if needed
5. Click **Send**

### Step 5: Track Results
1. Go to **Analytics** or **Overview**
2. Watch for:
   - 📩 Email opens (you'll get notified)
   - 🔗 Link clicks (you'll get notified)
   - Overall statistics

## 💡 Best Practices

### Writing Effective Emails
- ✅ Let AI write the first draft (it's professional and detailed)
- ✅ Personalize with recipient's name
- ✅ Mention something specific about them if possible
- ✅ Keep the call-to-action clear
- ✅ Follow up if they open but don't reply

### When to Follow Up
- If they **open** your email → Wait 2-3 days, then follow up
- If they **click** your link → They're interested! Follow up within 24 hours
- If they **don't open** → Try a different subject line

### Profile Tips
Your profile is used to personalize emails. Be specific:

**Bad Example:**
```
Product: My Tool
Description: A tool for creators
```

**Good Example:**
```
Product: ClipVoBooster
Description: AI-powered email marketing platform that helps businesses send professional emails with real-time tracking. Features include AI email generation, open/click tracking, and beautiful HTML templates.
Website: https://clipvo.site
```

## 📊 Understanding Analytics

### Key Metrics
- **Emails Sent**: Total emails you've sent
- **Opened**: How many recipients opened your email
- **Open Rate**: Percentage who opened (industry avg: 15-25%)
- **Clicks**: How many clicked links in your email
- **Click Rate**: Percentage who clicked (industry avg: 2-5%)

### What the Notifications Mean
- 📩 **"X opened your email"** → They're interested enough to read
- 🔗 **"X clicked a link"** → Hot lead! They want to learn more
- ✅ **"Email sent successfully"** → Confirmation it was delivered

## ⚠️ Important Notes

### Email Deliverability
- Don't send more than 50 emails/day from new account
- Warm up your account gradually
- Use professional subject lines
- Avoid spam words ("FREE", "GUARANTEE", etc.)

### Gmail Limits
- Free Gmail: 500 emails/day
- Google Workspace: 2,000 emails/day
- New accounts: Start with 50/day and increase gradually

### Best Practices
- ✅ Personalize when possible
- ✅ Send during business hours (Tue-Thu 10am-2pm best)
- ✅ Keep subject lines under 50 characters
- ✅ Test with your own email first
- ❌ Don't send to purchased email lists
- ❌ Don't send identical emails to everyone

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### "Emails not showing in dashboard"
- Refresh the page (it auto-refreshes every 5 seconds)
- Check you're logged in with correct account
- Wait 10-15 seconds after sending

### "Tracking not working"
- Make sure you deployed with correct `NEXT_PUBLIC_PROD_URL`
- Tracking requires production URL, not localhost
- Check browser console for errors

### "Gmail connection failed"
- Re-connect your Gmail account
- Make sure Gmail API is enabled in Google Cloud
- Check that redirect URI matches exactly

### "AI email generation failed"
- Check your OpenRouter API key
- Make sure you have API credits
- Fallback template will be used if AI fails
